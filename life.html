Life

Friedrich Nietzsche was born on October 15th, 1944 in the town of Rocken.

He was named after King Friedrich Wilhelm IV of Prussia.

Nietzsche went to school in Naumborg, where academic records show he excelled in Christian Theology.


This is ironic considering he would later denounce Christianity and believe Christians to be weak.

Nietzsche got into the prestigious Schulpforta, He wrote about Poets and became intrigued by non-German writers.
